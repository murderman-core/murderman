//*********** MURDERMAN: THE GAME *************\\

                    RULES
1. There is a murderer and an undercover person both have knives
2. The undercover's goal is to kill the murderer without killing a lot of innocents
3. Every minute there is a meeting where everyone in the game decides who to throw into the pit
4. The murderer's goal is to kill all the innocents and the undercover
5. No one knows who the murderer or the undercover is.
